📊 Chocolate Sales Data Analysis Dashboard

This project presents an interactive Chocolate Sales Data Analysis Dashboard built to uncover key business insights from sales data across different products, regions, and time periods.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of performance metrics such as total sales, total profit, product-wise sales distribution, and regional performance. It helps in identifying top-performing products, high-revenue regions, and overall business trends.

🔍 Key Highlights:
📈 Total Sales Analysis – Tracks overall sales (~24.45M) and performance trends
📦 Product Insights – Comparison of sales across different chocolate products
🌍 Geographical Analysis – Sales distribution across countries like Australia, India, Canada, UK, etc.
🧑‍💼 Salesperson Filter – Analyze performance by individual sales representatives
📊 Profit Breakdown – Visual representation of profit contribution by each product
📅 Time-based Filtering – Year-wise analysis (2023 & 2024)
📉 KPI Metrics – Includes total boxes sold, total amount, and overall profit
📌 Tools & Technologies Used:
Power BI (for dashboard creation & visualization)
Excel / CSV (data source)
Data Cleaning & Transformation
🎯 Objective:

To transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision making and improve business performance.
